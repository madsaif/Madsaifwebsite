About This App

Business Manager is a powerful, all-in-one application designed to help small and medium-sized businesses streamline their daily operations. From inventory tracking and client management to financial reporting and team collaboration, this app brings everything you need under one roof.

Built with flexibility in mind, Business Manager adapts to your workflow — not the other way around. Whether you run a retail shop, a service-based business, or an online store, this app equips you with the tools to manage efficiently and grow confidently.

Key Features

  • Sales tracking and revenue reports
  • Expense & invoice management
  • Team collaboration & task assignment
  • dashboard & analytics
